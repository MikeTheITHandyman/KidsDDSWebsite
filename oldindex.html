Frequently Asked Questions

When should my child have their first dental visit?

We recommend scheduling your child's first dental visit around their first birthday or within six months of their first tooth erupting.

Do you accept dental insurance?

Yes! We're in-network with most major PPO dental insurance plans and will help you maximize your benefits.

What should I expect at my child's first visit?

The first visit is usually short and focused on getting your child comfortable with the dental office. We'll do a gentle examination and discuss proper oral care.
